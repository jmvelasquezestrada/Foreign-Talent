Software Development Engineer
ASML - Taipei, Taiwan

Within ASML, the sector Development and Engineering (consisting of ca. 2500 engineers) is responsible for the specification, design, integration, qualification and sustaining of all ASML products.

While the core of the development programs is based at the ASML headquarters (HQ) in the Netherlands, an increasing number of project deliverables in the area of lithography and metrology applications are realized by the D&E Applications group of the ASML Center of Excellence (ACE) in Linkou, Taiwan. Taking advantage of customer proximity in Asia, the activities of the group involve customer (on-site) co-operation to develop (prototype) application software, perform proof-of-principle studies, and deliver best-known methods, as well as design and performance specifications for new application products.

  • Job Role: Technology / IT
  • Industry: Themes
  • Career Level: Mid Career

Responsibilities

  • Develop software applications for the semiconductor equipment field, specifically for metrology.
  • Translate requirements into SW solutions and continuously improve the code to meet customer’s needs and expectations.
  • Design, implement, test and maintain software system, mainly written in C#, C++ or Java.
  • Participate in integration tests on the system; analyze system issues that have been found during the integration tests.
  • Thoroughly document SW design, testing results and publish them internally.
  • Remotely align with HQ software development team on SW interfaces, version control, patch releases, debugging, etc. in a self-propelling manner.
